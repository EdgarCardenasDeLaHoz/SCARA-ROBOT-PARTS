# Summary

A simple 3d printed claw that I designed to be used with a 9g servo. It keeps the servo parallel to te claw action in order to reduce the weight in the arm.

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FNFmIbqV8nc

# Post-Printing

The arm itself consist in two pieces, I made it this way in order to make it easy to print and avoid delamination in the gear. After printing the two pieces must be glued together. 

For the circular gear I cut the center of one of the arms that comes with the servo and inserted it in the round gear. It should be a tight fit but it can also be glued for a better union.
